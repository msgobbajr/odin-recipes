Buffalo Chicken Pizza

Description

Quick buffalo chicken flatbread recipe. Filling and not too high in calories. Less then 15 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 Naan Flatbread
  • 1lb Cooked Shredded Chicken
  • 1/2 Cup Franks Nashville Hot Buffalo Sauce
  • 4oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Ranch Dressing
  • 1/2 Chopped Red Onion

Steps

  1. Place flatbreads in the oven at 400 for 7 minutes
  2. While flatbreads are crisping, mix chicken and hot sauce.
  3. Remove from oven and top flatbreads with ranch.
  4. Top with chicken mixture.
  5. Top with cheddar cheese and red onion.
  6. Place back in oven for 10 mintues or until cheese is melted.

Penne Vodka

Description

Penne vodka dish using pancetta and calabrian chilies to increase the flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Penne
  • Pancetta
  • Onion
  • Tomato Puree
  • Tomato Paste
  • Minced Garlic
  • Calabrian Chilies
  • Vodka
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil water in large pot and cook penne. Reserve water.
  2. Cook pancetta and remove from pan, leave grease in pan.
  3. Add onion, garlic, chilies, tomato paste to fat and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree and simmer for 30-45 minutes until thick.
  5. Add vodka to pan and cook off.
  6. Add noodles to sauce with pasta water and Parm.
  7. Add butter, pasta water, and salt until sauce is the right consistency.

Ground Beef Bulgogi

Description

Korean bulgogi inspired recipe made easy for a quick weeknight meal. Pairs great with cucumbers, siracha, mayo.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 3-4 cloves minced Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Minced Ginger
  • 1/2 Tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Tbsp Seasame Oil
  • 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 cups uncooked Jasmine Rice

Steps

  1. Cook jasmine rice in rice cooker.
  2. Heat oil in skillet over medium high heat.
  3. Add garlic and stir for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add beef and break apart, cook until browned.
  5. Meanwhile mix all other ingredients in bowl.
  6. Once beef is cooked, add sauce. Cook and stir for 2-3 minutes.
